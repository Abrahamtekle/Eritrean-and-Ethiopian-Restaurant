✦ Our Promise

Why Calgary Loves Us

Authentic food, genuine hospitality, and culture on every plate — every single day on Stephen Avenue.

🌿

100% Authentic Recipes

Traditional Eritrean and Ethiopian family recipes prepared the same way they have been for generations — no shortcuts, ever.

🍞

Freshly Made Injera

Our injera is fermented and prepared fresh in-house every morning using authentic teff flour — the real foundation of every plate.

Downtown Calgary Delivery

We deliver hot food across Calgary Downtown and surrounding neighbourhoods. Authentic East African cuisine at your door.

Vegan & Vegetarian Friendly

Over half our menu is fully plant-based. Ethiopian cuisine is naturally rich in vibrant, spiced vegan dishes for every taste.
